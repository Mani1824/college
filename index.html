Admissions Process

Simple steps to join
Bhagyaradhi

We've made our admissions process clear and hassle-free. From inquiry to your first day in class — guided at every step.

1
Step One

Enquire / Apply

Fill the online form or call our admissions desk. Share basic details about the student and preferred course.

2
Step Two

Counselling Session

Our academic counsellor meets with the student and parents to understand goals and recommend the right stream and course combination.

3
Step Three

Document Submission

Submit previous mark sheets, transfer certificate and ID proof. Our team verifies and processes within 1–2 working days.

4
Step Four

Confirm & Join

Complete fee payment, collect your ID card and course kit. Attend orientation and begin your journey with us.
